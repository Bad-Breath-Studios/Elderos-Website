Cookie Policy

Last updated: February 9, 2026

The Elderos website primarily uses localStorage rather than traditional HTTP cookies. This page explains what data is stored in your browser and why.

1. What Are Cookies & localStorage?

Cookies are small text files stored by your browser that are sent to the server with every request. localStorage is a browser storage mechanism that keeps data on your device without sending it to the server automatically. We primarily use localStorage because it is more efficient for our use case.

2. What We Store

The Elderos website stores the following data in your browser's localStorage:

Key Purpose Lifetime
elderos_token JWT authentication token — keeps you logged in across pages. Until logout or token expiry (24h)
elderos_user Cached account info (username, donator rank) — used to personalize the navbar. Until logout

3. Third-Party Cookies

We do not currently use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising cookies. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or similar services. If this changes in the future, we will update this policy and notify users.

4. Stripe (Payment Processing)

When you make a purchase through the Elderos launcher, Stripe may set its own cookies for fraud prevention and payment processing. These cookies are governed by Stripe's Privacy Policy and are not controlled by us.

5. Cloudflare

Our website is served through Cloudflare's CDN, which may set a __cf_bm cookie for bot management and DDoS protection. This is a strictly necessary security cookie and cannot be disabled. See Cloudflare's cookie policy for details.

6. Managing Your Data

You can clear all Elderos localStorage data at any time:

  • Click "Log Out" in the navbar — this removes all stored auth data.
  • Open your browser's Developer Tools (F12) → Application → Local Storage → clear the relevant domain.
  • Use your browser's "Clear browsing data" feature.

Clearing this data will log you out and reset any saved preferences.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as our technology evolves. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
