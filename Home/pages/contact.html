How to Reach Us

Our Discord server has dedicated channels for different types of communication:

Suggestions

Got an idea? Post it in our dedicated suggestions thread. The community votes on submissions, and the dev team reviews and approves or denies each one.

Feedback

Share your thoughts on recent updates, balance changes, or anything else about the game in our feedback channel. We read everything.

Support Tickets

Need help with your account, a payment issue, or want to appeal a punishment? Open a support ticket for private, one-on-one assistance from staff.

What to Include

To help us assist you faster, please include the following when reaching out:

  • Your username — So we can look up your account.
  • Description of the issue — Be as specific as possible.
  • Screenshots or logs — If applicable, these help us diagnose problems quickly.
  • Transaction ID — For payment-related inquiries, include the ID from your receipt email.
