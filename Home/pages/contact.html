What to Include

To help us assist you faster, please include the following when reaching out:

  • Your username — So we can look up your account.
  • Description of the issue — Be as specific as possible.
  • Screenshots or logs — If applicable, these help us diagnose problems quickly.
  • Transaction ID — For payment-related inquiries, include the ID from your receipt email.

Response Times

We aim to respond to all inquiries within:

  • Discord — Within 24 hours for support tickets.
  • Email — Within 48 hours for general inquiries.

Response times may be longer on weekends and holidays.
