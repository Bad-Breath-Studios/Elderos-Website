Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Elderos.

Getting Started
What is Elderos? ▼

Elderos is an Old School RuneScape private server (RSPS) focused on delivering an authentic OSRS experience with quality-of-life improvements, custom content, and a welcoming community. We support multiple game modes and world types.

How do I play? ▼

Download the Elderos Launcher from play.elderos.io. Create an account through the launcher, then log in and start playing. For a detailed walkthrough, see our Getting Started guide.

Is Elderos free to play? ▼

Yes! Elderos is completely free to play. We offer optional cosmetic purchases and donator ranks through the in-game store, but these are not required to enjoy the full game experience.

What game modes are available? ▼

Elderos supports multiple game modes:

  • Normal — Standard gameplay experience.
  • Ironman — Self-sufficient, no trading with other players.
  • Hardcore Ironman — One life. Death is permanent.
  • Ultimate Ironman — No bank. Carry everything.
  • Group Ironman — Ironman with a team.
Account & Security
How do I enable two-factor authentication (2FA)? ▼

You can enable 2FA through the Elderos Launcher under Account Settings. You'll need an authenticator app like Google Authenticator or Authy to scan the QR code. Once enabled, you'll need to enter a 6-digit code each time you log in.

I forgot my password. What do I do? ▼

Contact our support team on Discord for account recovery assistance. You'll need to verify your identity to reset your password.

Can I change my username? ▼

Yes! You can change your username through the Elderos Launcher under Account Settings.

Donations & Store
What are Eldercoins? ▼

Eldercoins are the virtual currency used in the Elderos store. You can purchase them through the launcher and spend them on donator ranks, cosmetic items, and other perks.

Can I get a refund? ▼

Purchases of digital goods are generally final. Refunds may be issued for duplicate charges, undelivered items, or unauthorized purchases. See our full Refund Policy for details.

Do donator ranks give gameplay advantages? ▼

Donator ranks provide quality-of-life perks and cosmetic benefits. We strive to keep the game balanced — donator perks do not provide direct combat advantages or items that cannot be obtained through normal gameplay.

Rules & Moderation
What are the rules? ▼

Full rules are outlined in our Terms of Service. The short version: no botting, no bug abuse, no real-world trading, no harassment, and be respectful to other players and staff.

I was banned. Can I appeal? ▼

Ban appeals can be submitted through a support ticket on our Discord server. Provide your username and a detailed explanation. Appeals are reviewed by staff and responses are not guaranteed.

How do I report a player or bug? ▼

You can report players in-game using the right-click report option, or through a support ticket on Discord. For bugs, use the bug-reports channel on Discord.
