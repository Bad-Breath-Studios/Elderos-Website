How It Works

Our affiliate program rewards content creators and community members who bring new players to Elderos. Share your unique referral link, and earn in-game rewards for every purchase made by players you refer.

Simple deal: When someone signs up through your link and makes a purchase in the Elderos store, you earn rewards based on the sale value. For every $1 spent by your referral, you receive 100 Eldercoins and $1 towards your donator rank. For example, a $5 purchase by a referred player earns you 500 Eldercoins + $5 in donator rank credits.

Getting Started

  1. Create an account — Download the Elderos launcher and register your account.
  2. Apply on Discord — Open a ticket in our Discord server and request affiliate access. Include your Elderos username and any content platforms you use.
  3. Get your link — Once approved, you'll receive a unique referral link and access to the affiliate dashboard on the Creators page.
  4. Start earning — Share your link in videos, streams, social media, or wherever your audience hangs out. Track clicks, signups, and earnings in real time.

Rules & Guidelines

  • No spam. Don't mass-DM, bot, or use misleading ads to drive referrals.
  • No self-referrals. Creating alt accounts through your own link will get you removed.
  • Be honest. Represent Elderos accurately — don't make promises we can't keep.
  • Cookie duration: Referral cookies last 30 days. If a referred player makes a purchase within 30 days of clicking your link, you get credit.
  • Rewards are in-game only. This program does not offer real-money compensation. Rewards are Eldercoins and donator rank credits applied to your Elderos account.
  • Monthly credits: Earnings are credited to your account on the 1st of each month.

FAQ

Who can join?

Anyone with an active Elderos account. Content creators, streamers, community leaders, or just players who love the game and want to share it.

How are referrals tracked?

Each affiliate gets a unique link (e.g., elderos.io/?ref=yourname). When someone clicks it, a 30-day cookie is set. Any account created and purchases made during that window are attributed to you.

What do I earn?

For every dollar a referred player spends, you receive 100 Eldercoins (spendable in the store) and $1 in donator rank credits (counts toward your donator rank progression). Higher tiers earn a bonus percentage on top of the base rate. All rewards are in-game only — no real-money payouts.

When do rewards get credited?

Eldercoins and donator credits are applied to your Elderos account on the 1st of each month. You can spend Eldercoins immediately in the store.

Can I lose my affiliate status?

Yes. Violating the guidelines above, inactivity for 90+ days, or abusive behavior will result in removal from the program. We'll always reach out before taking action.
