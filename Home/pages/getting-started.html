Getting Started

New to Elderos? Follow these steps to get into the game.

System Requirements

Elderos runs on most modern computers. You'll need:

  • Windows 10/11 or macOS 12+
  • 4 GB RAM minimum
  • Java 23+ (bundled with launcher)
  • Stable internet connection

Setup Guide

1

Download the Launcher

Head to play.elderos.io and download the Elderos Launcher for your operating system. The launcher handles everything — client updates, account management, and the in-game store.

2

Create an Account

Open the launcher and click "Create Account." Choose a username and password. Your username will be your in-game display name, so pick wisely. We recommend using a unique password that you don't use elsewhere.

3

Enable Two-Factor Authentication

After creating your account, go to Account Settings in the launcher and enable 2FA. This adds an extra layer of security using an authenticator app like Google Authenticator or Authy. Scan the QR code and enter the 6-digit code to confirm.

Tip

Save your 2FA backup codes in a safe place. If you lose access to your authenticator app, these codes are the only way to recover your account.

4

Choose a World

Select a world from the world selector in the launcher. Economy worlds offer the standard experience, while PvP worlds add an extra layer of danger. Choose the region closest to you for the best connection.

5

Start Playing

Click "Play" and the launcher will download the latest client and connect you to the game. Create your character, choose your game mode, and begin your adventure in Gielinor.

First Steps In-Game

Once you're logged in, here are some things to do:

  • Talk to the Starter Guide — Located at the spawn point. They'll give you starter equipment and explain the basics.
  • Explore the home area — Find shops, banks, altars, and teleportation portals.
  • Set up your bank PIN — Protect your items with an extra layer of security.
  • Join the Discord — Connect with the community for tips, trading, and updates.
  • Check the Wiki — Our Wiki has guides for skills, bosses, quests, and more.
