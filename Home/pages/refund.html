Refund Policy

Last updated: February 9, 2026

This policy outlines the conditions under which refunds may be issued for purchases made through the Elderos in-game store. All payments are processed by Stripe.

All purchases on Elderos are for digital goods and are generally considered final once delivered to your account.

1. Digital Goods

Elderos offers digital goods including, but not limited to:

  • Eldercoins — Virtual currency used within the game.
  • Donator Ranks — Account upgrades that unlock perks and cosmetics.
  • Cosmetic Items — Visual customizations for your character.

These items are delivered instantly to your account upon successful payment. Because of the instant-delivery nature of digital goods, all purchases are considered final.

2. Refund Eligibility

We may issue a refund in the following circumstances:

  • Duplicate charge — You were charged multiple times for the same purchase.
  • Items not delivered — Payment was processed but the items were not credited to your account due to a technical error.
  • Unauthorized purchase — Someone made a purchase on your account without your authorization (evidence required).

Refund requests must be made within 14 days of the original purchase.

3. Refund Exclusions

Refunds will not be issued in the following cases:

  • You changed your mind after purchasing.
  • You spent or consumed the purchased items (e.g., Eldercoins used in the store).
  • Your account was banned or suspended for violating the Terms of Service.
  • You transferred items purchased with Eldercoins to another player.
  • More than 14 days have passed since the purchase.

4. Chargebacks & Disputes

Filing a chargeback or payment dispute with your bank or payment provider without first contacting us will result in an immediate and permanent ban of your account. If you believe a charge is incorrect, please contact us first so we can resolve the issue.

Always contact us before disputing a charge with your bank. We are happy to resolve legitimate issues quickly.

5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  1. Join our Discord server.
  2. Open a support ticket in the appropriate channel.
  3. Provide your username, transaction ID (from your email receipt), and a description of the issue.
  4. Our team will review your request and respond within 72 hours.

6. Refund Processing

Approved refunds are processed through Stripe back to the original payment method. Processing times depend on your bank or card provider, but typically take 5-10 business days after approval.

When a refund is issued, the corresponding digital items will be removed from your account. If items have already been used, we may deduct the equivalent value from your account balance.

7. Account Termination

If your account is terminated for violating our Terms of Service, you are not entitled to a refund for any prior purchases. Maintaining account access is contingent on following the rules.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy at any time. Changes take effect upon posting. Continued purchases after changes are posted constitute acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact

For refund inquiries, contact us via our Discord server or Contact page.

This Refund Policy was last updated on February 9, 2026.
