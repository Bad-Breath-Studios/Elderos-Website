About Elderos

OSRS Done Right.

Our Story

Elderos was born from a simple idea: what if Old School RuneScape could be experienced the way it was meant to be — with modern infrastructure, thoughtful quality-of-life improvements, and a community that actually matters?

Built by a small team of passionate OSRS players and developers at Bad Breath Studios, Elderos is a labor of love. Every feature, every line of code, and every design choice is made with one goal in mind: creating the best possible RSPS experience.

What Makes Us Different

We're not just another RSPS. Elderos is built from the ground up with professional-grade architecture:

  • Modern infrastructure — Multi-world support, real-time monitoring, and automated deployments.
  • Security first — BCrypt passwords, JWT authentication, two-factor auth, and encrypted local storage.
  • Custom launcher — Purpose-built desktop application for account management, the in-game store, and automatic updates.
  • Active development — Regular updates, bug fixes, and new content driven by community feedback.
  • Transparent moderation — Staff tools with full audit logging and role-based access control.

Our Values

Authenticity

We respect the OSRS experience. Our content additions complement the game rather than replacing what makes it great.

Quality

We'd rather release one polished feature than ten half-finished ones. Every update is tested, refined, and built to last.

Community

Our players shape the direction of the game. Feedback drives our roadmap, and every voice matters.

Fairness

No pay-to-win. Donator perks are cosmetic and quality-of-life — never gameplay advantages over free players.

Bad Breath Studios

Bad Breath Studios is the independent development team behind Elderos. We're a small group of developers, designers, and gamers who believe that private servers deserve the same level of care and craftsmanship as commercial titles.
